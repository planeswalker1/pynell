About Pynell

Founded in 2018, we are an independent and growing American web studio. We are committed to building the next generation of websites.

Since we started our mission has been simple: to build generation-defining websites. To us, a generation-defining website consists of more than just a great website. They have a lasting impact on an entire sector of the population. They have a cultural impact, bring people together, create memories that will last a lifetime and push the entire medium of websites forward.

Generation-defining websites are few and far between. We have shaped our entire organization in a manner that we think will allow us to create websites of that caliber. We do not follow trends or emulate what others have done. We have a flat and open corporate structure that promotes the sharing and exploration of new, innovative and creative ideas.
